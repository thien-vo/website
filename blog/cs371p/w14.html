What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the week?

My-pick-of-the week is MightyText. It's an application that allows you to text from your computer through Chrome extension or its web application. Here's my personal review for the app, compared to another one called "MySMS". There are also Pushbullet and AirDroid, and you can read a more professional review here. 

                Mighty Text
Pros:
	- Wide-range of control in additions to texting
	- Syncing photos/videos (anything taken)
	- Also manage your gallery from chrome
	- Manage apps (uninstall quickly, easily, even the ones without uninstall buttons)
	- Syncing battery (fun)
	- User-friendly, easy-to-use UI
	- Some selections of themes
	- Phone locator (help find phone in the house)
	- Can send long messages (up to 1000 words) and it will automatically separate or do whatever it needs to get the message through
Cons:
	- Most of the things stated above are mostly available for only PRO version
	- Limited platforms (Chrome extension only ?)
	- Expensive premium ($60/y or $7/m) - sometimes have promo ($3.5 for 6m)
	- Draining battery when use other features (untested)
	- 250 msg/ month on free account
	- Glitches of not sending messages on some phones

MySMS
Pros:
	- Cheap premium ($10/y)
	- Lightweight, simple and neat UI
	- Cross-platform (Windows, Android, Mac, etc)
Cons:
	- Sending "via mysms.com" for free version
	- Cut off after 160, giving error "exceeded maximum of characters"
  - No monthly limits on free account (!?)
