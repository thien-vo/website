CS371p Spring 2017: Thien Vo

Weekly blog for Object-Orieted Programming

Week 1: 01/16/2017 - 01/22/2017

What did you do this past week?

Since it was the first week, I spent most of my time organizing syllabuses and schedules. I always bookmark all my classes' websites, canvas or piazza sites, and other materials such as syllabuses or class's schedule. This helps me greatly for the past semesters. It provide me quick access to all the material I'd need whether I'm on my phone or a computer. Also, I've been trying to adjust to my new school/work schedule. I must say that I have a pretty consistent schedule: 10-5 every day, mixed between work and school throughout every day.

What's in your way?

As of now, I don't have anything significantly troubling me yet. I just need to start keeping up with the works in all my classes and make sure that I do not fall behind.

What will you do next week?

I intend to work on my personal project in my free time, called Beauty Flow. I suspect that I will have my first project and/or assignment for every one of my class, so I will need to prioritize that first. Also, I need to start learning the basic of Haskell for my programming languages class. My professor requested the students to study the basics on our own.

What's my experience of the class?

I don't know many good friends in the class, but that makes me feel more excited about the class. I will have the opportunity to get to know more people and expand my network. Friday class was definitely intriguing to learn about how operators work in different languages. Although I feel it was just a brief overview, I definitely like the quick-paced lecture style of Professor Downing. Also, the way he calls on people to answer and engage into his lecture makes the lecture more direct and straightforward.

What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the week?

My tip-of-the-week is that you guys should bookmark all of your classes' sites, syllabus, or any other material to your browser. There will be time that you just want to do the reading while hanging in the doctor office or just quickly review the schedule of the week. With the bookmark, you guys don't have to google "CS 371p" or such any more.
