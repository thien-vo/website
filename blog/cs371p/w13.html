What did you do this past week?

This past week, I finished an essay for my culture class and the last project in OOP. Also, I started to run and bike again over the weekend. I ran for 4 miles on Saturday and bike for 6-8 miles on Sunday. I have started to study for my classes though, because I don't want to spend my entire coming weekend to study for the final exams.

What's in your way?

I have to catch up on Probability a lot, and I don't like that. However, I will get through that just fine. I have 3 exams on the same day 2 weeks from now, so I am trying to space out my studies so I don't have to cram it all at once.

What will you do next week?

First, I will need to catch up on Probability. Second, I will need to start making a study guide for OOP. There has been a lot of new material that the class has covered since the last exam. I also plan on running at least 3-4 times this coming week. I want to get back in shape for the summer. I will need that stamina for 8-hour work days during the summer.

What's my experience of the class?

It has been a quite slow week in my opinion. I was very distracted during class time because the material was a little bit repetitive from the last project. However, Prof. Downing keeps giving new and interesting facts/lessons about C++ that the overall lectures are still quite engaging.

What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the week?

My pick-of-the-week is the site/group called ProductHunt. They give you updates/news on technology, especially startups. It also provides a platform for startups to advertise themselves and get their names out there. Subscribe to them to stay up-to-date with new technologies.
