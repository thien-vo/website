Week 10: 03/27/2017 - 04/02/2017

What did you do this past week?

This past week, I completed my two midterms: Probability and Texas Government. I think I did fairly well on both of them, so I am pretty happy with the effort that I put into it last week. I also found my partner for coming OOP project, and we started working on it over this weekend. In fact, we are close to finish. I also had a technical interview with MaxPoint, and they want me to come to the office this coming Wednesday for the final round. I am so excited for it, and I need to get ready for it too.

What's in your way?

Other than OOP project, I just have some competitive programming homeworks and probability homework to catch up on. Then, I need to prepare myself for MaxPoint final round of interviews.

What will you do next week?

I plan on spending at least several hours to review projects that I put on my resume, so that I am well-prepared for MaxPoint. Also, I want to do my homework for probability by Tuesday, so I don't have to worry about it. No more procrastination, I promise.

What's my experience of the class?

We had a talk with Atlassian. I think the talk was rather long and too personal. The speakers were presenting the problems they encountered as a developer, but to be honest, most CS students have encounter some sort of similar problems when work in a group project anyway. So instead of trying to make something so typical important, I think the speakers could have told us more about any graduate programs or internship programs. They did cover it, but it was very short and lack details.

What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the week?

My pick-of-the-week is Attack on Titan anime series. I have been waited for season 2 to come out for over a year. They released it on several networks on April's Fool day. I thought it was a joke, but I was so happy that it wasn't!
