CS371p Spring 2017: Thien Vo

Weekly blog for Object-Oriented Programming

Week 11: 04/03/2017 - 04/09/2017

What did you do this past week?

This past week, I had an on-site interview with MaxPoint. This is the first on-site interview ever for me. It went really well. I was interview by 3 interviewers: 2 engineers and a SE manager. The first engineer gives me a technical problem to solve. It took me a little bit of thinking to work it out, but I solved it at the end. Then, he explained to me more of what MaxPoint does and what his team contributes to the company. The 2nd interviewer is the manager, and he asked me a lot of resume-based questions. The 3rd interviewer asked me more situational questions about working on projects and work ethics. It was quite intense for me personally, but I feel good once I've finished all 3 interviews in an hour and a half. On the same day, I received an email from the HR asking to speak with me on the next day to see if I have a preference for a team over the other. Some of my friends told me that I got the offer, but I was just still skeptical. However, the next day, she told me that all of the interviewers think that I would be a good part on the team, and they offer me an intern position in either Big Data or High Scalability team. I chose Big Data, and I will be starting working with them starting early June. I am looking forward to it.
